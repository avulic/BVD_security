Who We Are

B.V.D. Security

B.V.D. Security Ltd. is an elite, licensed private security agency based in Zagreb. We specialize in high-risk operations and providing protection to individuals who require absolute security without compromising privacy.

Our operational core consists of experienced professionals from the MUP special units and experts with years of experience in the security sector. Our approach is not just reactive; we analyze, anticipate, and neutralize risks before they become a threat.
